Features

With the many features of PassionFruit, you'll find your next business partner, team, or volunteer for your organization.

100% Free

The app is available to all at zero cost to the user. Download with a smartphone, and begin to plant the seeds so that you grow your PassionFruit!

Workspaces

Find workspaces in the area that are known to breed productivity and entrepreneurship. A list of Cafés, libraries, innovation spaces and more are provided to you so you and your new partners can meet in an ideal workspace.

Fully Responsive

Don't want to use your phone? Our experienced App developers are currently working on a fully functioning PassionFruit desktop app that works on Mac and Windows computers

Networking

Find a list of interest-specific events in the area that will help you increase your network and grow your PassionFruit.
